# Inventory_Data_Analysis
# 📊 Inventory Management Dashboard (Excel)

## 🔹 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Inventory Management Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel to analyze stock performance, demand patterns, and supply chain efficiency. The dashboard enables interactive exploration of inventory data and supports data-driven decision-making for optimizing operations.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to:

* Monitor inventory levels and sales performance
* Identify stockout risks and demand trends
* Evaluate supplier and warehouse efficiency
* Provide actionable insights for inventory optimization

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Slicers & Timeline Filters
* VBA (for dashboard automation)

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset consists of **2000 rows of simulated inventory data** with the following attributes:

* Date
* Product ID & Product Name
* Category
* Warehouse
* Opening Stock
* Units Received
* Units Sold
* Closing Stock
* Stockout Flag
* Lead Time (Days)
* Supplier
* Unit Cost & Unit Price

---

## 📊 Key Features of Dashboard

* Interactive **KPI Cards** for quick performance overview
* Dynamic filtering using **Slicers (Category, Warehouse, Supplier)**
* **Timeline filter** for time-based analysis
* Multiple visualizations including:

  * Monthly Sales Trend
  * Category-wise Sales Distribution
  * Warehouse Performance
  * Supplier Lead Time Analysis
  * Stockout Analysis

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Units Sold
* Average Opening & Closing Stock
* Inventory Turnover
* Stockout Rate
* Stockout Days

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Total sales reached **136,024 units**, indicating steady demand with seasonal variation
* High inventory turnover reflects **efficient stock utilization**
* Stockout rate is low (**1.1%**), but still impacts key product categories
* **Food and Stationery** are top-performing categories, while **Furniture shows low movement**
* **Nagoya Depot leads in sales**, highlighting regional demand differences
* Supplier lead times vary, indicating **inconsistent supplier performance**
* Higher stockouts in **Electronics and Clothing** suggest need for improved forecasting

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Improve demand forecasting for high-variability categories
* Optimize stock allocation across warehouses
* Reduce dependency on high lead-time suppliers
* Maintain higher safety stock for fast-moving products
